What Drives My Creativity

Art

Art has always been at the center of Leigh's life. From a young age, she was drawn to painting and other creative outlets as a way to express herself and make sense of the world around her. It wasn't just something she enjoyed, it was something that shaped how she thought and who she wanted to become. That early passion for art would eventually guide many of her decisions, influencing both her education and her career path.

Coding

As her path evolved, Leigh found a way to carry that same creativity into a new medium. After facing health challenges that forced her to step away from art school, she shifted her focus toward web design and development. Through her studies at Bellevue University, she learned how to combine visual creativity with technical problem-solving. Today, she brings those skills together in her work as a front-end developer and graphic designer, using code as another form of creative expression. She is currently working hard to become more proficient in her back-end development skills to add to her all-around skill set.

Writing

One of the most personal parts of Leigh's creative life is her writing. Since high school, she has been developing stories that she is now actively working to bring to life. Her projects include a three-part Weird West series with dark, psychological, and paranormal elements, as well as a medieval fantasy with a mystery aspect. While these stories are still in progress, they represent a long-term goal she has carried with her for years, and a commitment to telling stories in her own voice.

“Creativity is the thread that connects everything I do.”
